My Work

"Outta the Blue"

Check out my band's debut studio album, "Outta the Blue". I personally produced, mixed, and mastered every track on this album and featured on several of the tracks. Go stream it on Spotify, Apple Music, etc. today.

This album was the result of hundreds of hours of work throughout the summer of 2019. It features our first three singles Titanic, CONQUISTADORS, and CHECKMATE as well as seven other new songs. These three songs were also the first three tracks I ever produced and mixed and were created in December 2018, March 2019, and May 2019 respectively.

Milo Wolf - "TSX"

I mixed and mastered the three tracks above for Milo Wolf. Be sure to give him a follow on SoundCloud to stay updated.
