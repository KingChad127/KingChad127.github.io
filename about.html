About Abhinav

I'm Abhinav Chadaga, a 16 year old producer, mix engineer, and musician, based in Flower Mound, Texas. I'm also part of the rap trio INDIGOS and I produce, mix, and sing for the band. My producing style varies with each song I craft, but I generally like to blend elements from trap, R&B, and old-school hip hop to create energetic and unique compositions.

While I've only been producing and mixing music for a year, my background in music is extensive. My roots as a classically trained pianist and orchestral violinist for over eight years consistently shapes my work and drives me to create perfection. My passion for music runs deep and I hope to that together, we can create the next big hit.
