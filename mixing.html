Recording

I record artists using a large diaphragm condenser mic, the Audio-Technica AT-2035, which provides exceptional detail and resolution in recordings. Vocal tracking is done in a closet with acoustic treatment to reduce room noise and reflections. If you would like to hear raw vocals with this mic, click below.

Mixing

I mix in the box using the latest software plugins and analog emulations to achieve a crisp, full sounding mix. My tracking and mixing software of choice is Studio One 4 which I use to edit and comp vocal takes, balance drums and instruments, and mix vocals using premium equalizers and compression emulations, all in order to bring out the best qualities of your song. I utilize plugins from vendors like Waves, Slate Digital, Plugin Alliance, and more to achieve the right sound for the mix.

Mastering

I offer mastering services along with mixing services. If you get a project mixed by me, I offer mastering services free of charge. I use plugins like Izotope's Ozone 9 to create a clean, full sounding master that competes well with comercial mixes on streaming services. To achieve the perfect tonal and level balance, I run each master through Spotify's algorithm to ensure that tracks sound in place on all major streaming platforms.
